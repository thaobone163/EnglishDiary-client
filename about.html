About EuroKids

 About EuroKids

EuroKids International Ltd is the largest education services provider in the Pre-School segment and one of the largest children’s book publishing companies. EuroKids specialises in early childhood education, preschool programs and our commitment to the cause has made us the leading provider of Pre-School education. We have achieved this distinction both in the number of Pre-Schools we operate and the number of students we serve.

Since 2001, our first groups of Pre-School children have grown and many of their parents made clear to us their expectation that EuroKids should develop further, with their children, into a new environment. EuroSchool has taken birth out of that expectation. With thirteen operational primary and secondary schools and four more slated to start off by June 2010, EuroSchool aims to launch 30 more schools by 2012.
